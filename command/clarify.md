Clarify specifications using structured Spec-Driven Development methodology:

## Clarification Focus:
- Identify ambiguous or incomplete requirements
- Ask targeted questions for missing details
- Document assumptions and constraints
- Validate user story completeness
- Resolve conflicts or contradictions

## Process:
1. Review existing specifications
2. Identify gaps and ambiguities
3. Ask structured clarification questions
4. Document answers and decisions
5. Update specifications accordingly

## Output:
Updates `.specify/specs/[feature-name]/spec.md` with clarifications section.

Use todowrite to track clarification tasks and decisions made.
